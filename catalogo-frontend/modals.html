Guía de Modales con Tailwind CSS

Estructura Básica

fixed inset-0 z-50 bg-black bg-opacity-50 flex items-center justify-center transform scale-95 opacity-0

Animaciones

transform transition-all duration-300 scale-95 opacity-0 hover:scale-100 hover:opacity-100 translate-x-full

Tamaños

max-w-sm (small) max-w-md (medium) max-w-lg (large) max-w-2xl (xlarge)

Botones de Cerrar

text-gray-400 hover:text-gray-600 w-6 h-6 M6 18L18 6M6 6l12 12 onclick="toggleModal(id)"

Formularios

border border-gray-200 focus:ring-2 focus:ring-blue-500 rounded-xl space-y-4

Efectos Especiales

backdrop-blur-xl bg-white/10 border border-white/20 rounded-l-3xl
